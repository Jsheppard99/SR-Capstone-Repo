Safety Tips

  • Limit Personal Information Sharing
    Avoid sharing sensitive details (like Social Security numbers or bank information) online.
    Be cautious about what you post on social media
  • Be Careful with links
    Hover over links to see the URL before clicking
    Avoid clicking on links in unsolicited emails or texts
  • Log out of shared devices
    If using a shared computer at a library or friends house, make sure to log out of your accounts
    Leaving accounts logged in could expose sensitive information to others.
  • Keep software and apps updated
    Many updates include security patches that help protect against new threats.
    Make sure automatic updates are enabled when possible.
  • Secure Browsing
    Only enter personal information on websites that start with "https://" (the "s" means secure).
    Look for a padlock icon in the address bar
