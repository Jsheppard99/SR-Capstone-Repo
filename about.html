About Us

We are a group of IT students who came together for our senior design project with a shared goal: to create a user-friendly web app tailored for elderly individuals. Our mission is to empower seniors by providing accessible resources and information, making the internet a safer and more navigable space for everyone. We believe in enhancing digital literacy and safety for older adults, ensuring they can enjoy all the benefits the online world has to offer.
